What is PRP?  (Platelet-Rich Plasma)
PRP Hair Restoration therapy is an all-natural and non-invasive procedure that has been found to stimulate the growth of hair and to reduce the effects of thinning.
1. A small amount of blood is drawn.
2. PRP (platelet-rich plasma) is separated from the blood.
3. PRP concentrate is extracted.
4. The concentrated PRP is injected painlessly into the scalp with a small needle.
5. The hair follicles are stimulated and grow stronger.

*Zero Downtime
*Zero Side Effects
*Effective and Affordable
*Non-Surgical Procedure
